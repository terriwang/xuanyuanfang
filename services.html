Qimen Dunjia
奇門遁甲
HK$1,300Minimum

Qimen Dunjia, together with Tai Yi Shen Shu and Liu Ren Shen Shu, forms what is known as the "Three Ancient Arts" — historically applied as imperial statecraft, and specifically on the battlefield, to outmanoeuvre and defeat an opponent. Applied today, its scope in business and strategy remains just as broad.

In a commercial setting, it can be used to assess the likely outcome of a specific course of action and the factors to watch for. Where the outlook is unfavourable, this allows preparation well in advance, and the development of a counter-strategy before commitments are made.

Typically used for

  • A single, specific business question
  • A full analysis of internal and external circumstances
  • An in-depth review of the factors behind success or failure, with a tailored strategy to follow
