About Us

Vision, judgement, and a decade of practice.

玄圓坊 · 關於我們

玄圓坊 exists to bring structure to decisions that feel too complex, too personal, or too high-stakes to leave to instinct alone.

Vision

Why we exist.

Most people encounter classical Chinese metaphysics as folklore — interesting, perhaps decorative, rarely something a serious organisation would build a strategy around. We started 玄圓坊 to challenge that assumption.

These frameworks were, in their own time, the analytical tools of statesmen, generals, and merchants making decisions with real consequences. We treat them the same way today: as structured, testable methods for understanding timing, position, and risk — sitting alongside, not instead of, conventional strategic thinking.

Our long-term vision is a practice that moves fluidly between boardroom and personal counsel — equally comfortable advising on a project's timing as on a life decision — because the underlying discipline of judgement is the same in both.

“We are building a practice for people who want a second, disciplined perspective — not a prediction, and not a performance.”

Values

What we hold to, in every engagement.

I.

Discretion

Every engagement is confidential by default. We are advisors, not commentators — our clients' decisions are their own to share, or not.

II.

Rigour

Classical frameworks are applied with discipline and cross-checked against real-world context — never offered as a substitute for due diligence.

III.

Long-term partnership

The questions that matter most rarely resolve in a single conversation. We favour ongoing relationships over one-off readings.

Experience

Founder background.

Principal Consultant

Our founder's practice began over a decade ago, at the intersection of two disciplines rarely taught together: classical Chinese metaphysics and modern organisational strategy. That combination remains the foundation of every engagement at 玄圓坊.

Trained through years of formal study in BaZi, Qi Men Dun Jia, and Feng Shui under established lineage practitioners, and shaped further by direct commercial experience advising developers, executives, and family businesses across the Asia-Pacific region.

The conviction behind 玄圓坊 is straightforward: the frameworks are only as valuable as the judgement applied to them. That judgement is built from years of client work — private consultations, project reviews, and long-term advisory relationships — not from theory alone.
